About UPAO Performing Arts Center

Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and enrich our community through exceptional performing arts experiences that celebrate diversity and artistic excellence.

Our History

Established in 1983, UPAO has been a cornerstone of cultural arts for over four decades, bringing world-class performances to our community.

Our Venues

State-of-the-art facilities including a 1,200-seat main hall, intimate studio theater, and outdoor pavilion for diverse performance needs.
